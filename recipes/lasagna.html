Lasagna

Indulge in the comforting flavors of homemade lasagna with this easy recipe: Ingredients:

  • lb ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
  • 15 oz tomato sauce
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan

Instructions: Brown meat, onion, and garlic; add tomatoes, paste, sauce, herbs, salt, and pepper. Layer noodles, meat sauce, béchamel (optional), and cheeses in a baking dish.

Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes covered, then 10-15 minutes uncovered. Rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy a quick and delicious homemade lasagna!
