Simple Ground Beef Pasta

Savor the flavors of a quick and delightful pasta dish with ground beef:

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb pasta (of your choice)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for topping
Instructions:
  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, brown ground beef with onions and garlic.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta in the beef sauce until well coated.
  5. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan.

Enjoy a flavorful and fuss-free ground beef pasta!
